Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the frequently asked questions

What is perfume?
Perfume is a fragrant liquid made from essential oils, aroma compounds, and solvents. It is used to give a pleasant scent to the body, clothes, or surroundings.
How should perfume be applied correctly?
Perfume should be applied to pulse points such as the wrists, neck, behind the ears, and inner elbows for better diffusion and longer-lasting scent.
What are fragrance notes in perfume?
Fragrance notes are the different layers of scent in a perfume, including top notes, middle (heart) notes, and base notes that appear over time.
What is the difference between Eau de Parfum and Eau de Toilette?
Eau de Parfum has a higher concentration of fragrance oils and lasts longer, while Eau de Toilette is lighter and more suitable for everyday use.
How long does perfume usually last?
The longevity of a perfume depends on its concentration, ingredients, and skin type, but most perfumes last between 4 to 8 hours.
Can perfume be used daily?
Yes, perfume can be used daily as long as it is applied in moderation and suits your skin and environment.
How should perfume be stored?
Perfume should be kept in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat to preserve its quality.
